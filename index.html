Dr. Nnamdi Azikiwe
President of Nigeria from 1 October 1963 to 16 January 1966

  1. Nnamide Azikiwe established the University of Nigeria, Nsukka in 1960
  2. Azikiwe was inducted into the Agbalanze society of Onitsha as Nnanyelugo in 1946 a recognition for Onitsha men with significant accomplishments.
  3. In 1962, he became a second-rank red cap chieftain (Ndichie Okwa) as the Oziziani Obi.
  4. Chief Azikiwe was installed as the Owelle-Osowa-Anya of Onitsha in 1972, making him a first-rank hereditary red cap nobleman (Ndichie Ume) in the Igbo branch of the Nigerian chieftaincy system.
  5. Queen Elizabeth II appointed him to the Privy Council of the United Kingdom
  6. He was made Grand Commander of the Federal Republic (GCFR), Nigeria's highest national honour, in 1980.
  7. He founded Zik's Athletic Club (ZAC) which would open its doors to sportsmen and women of all races, nationalities, tribes, and classes of Nigeria
  8. Azikiwe became governor-general on 16 November 1960
  9. Azikiwe was elected to the Legislative Council in a Lagos municipal election from the National Democratic Party (an NCNC subsidiary)
  10. He became founding editor of the African Morning Post (a new daily newspaper in Accra, Ghana)
